# Comic Style Settings

## Art Style
- Style: Kyoto Animation Style, delicate characterization, clear eyes, soft lighting and shadows
- Language: English
- Sound Effects: Japanese manga style onomatopoeia

## Layout
- Direction: Vertical comic strip
- Panels per strip: 4-6 panels
- Speech Bubbles: Circular dialog boxes

## Characters
- Proportion: Normal (5-7 heads tall)
- Expressions: Moderately exaggerated

## Tone
- Genre: Relaxed and humorous, campus/workplace daily life
- Pacing: Medium, balanced dialogue and action

## Scenes
- Background: Simplified but recognizable
- Time: Modern
